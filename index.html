 What is this?

Rank Up! is a calculator that finds the best dungeons to run, based on the EXP you need and/or the Stamina you have. Plug in some numbers to give it a whirl.
Results will magically:
  • Take into account Daily Bonus events (such as 1/2 stamina).
  • Only suggest dungeons you have access to (customizable in Settings).
